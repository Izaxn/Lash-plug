Prep & Maintain

Lash Care Routine

A little care before and after keeps your set fuller, longer, and looking freshly plugged in.

🧴

Before Your Appointment

  • Arrive with a clean, makeup-free face — especially the eye area.
  • Wash lashes with an oil-free, lash-safe cleanser the morning of.
  • Remove all mascara, eyeliner, and eyeshadow beforehand.
  • Skip the caffeine — it helps you stay still and relaxed.
  • Come alone; no extra guests or little ones, please.
💎

Aftercare

  • Keep lashes completely dry for the first 24 hours.
  • Cleanse daily with an oil-free lash cleanser and a spoolie brush.
  • Avoid oil-based products, steam, and saunas near the eyes.
  • Brush lashes each morning to keep them fanned and tangle-free.
  • Book your fill within 2–3 weeks to keep the set full.
